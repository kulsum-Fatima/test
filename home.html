SPORTS PROMOTION FOUNDATION

The aim of the foundation is to be able to promote sports in India.

Sports Promotion Foundation, as the name stands is a non profit trust which has been working meticulously for the development and betterment of Sports in India. Founded by Educationist and Philanthropist Dr.Amit bhalla ( Vice President - Manav Rachna Group), Rajeev Gandhi Khel Ratna Awardee, Former World Record Holder, Ace Shooter, Olympian Ronjan Sodhi and promoted by Veteran Cricketer and Coach Mr. Sarkar Talwar, SPF's mission is to be able to promote sports in our nation from the grass root to the elite level. We aim at providing training solutions to players, organise events in India which match international standards, utilise existing sports infrastructure as well as build infrastructure to promote sports in all the sectors.
